Syntax

Use native, semantic HTML(5) tags. Screen readers know how to identify elements such as <header> and <nav>, as compared to generic <div> and <span> tags.

Headings should be in their natural hierarchy on the page. (Ex. <h1> followed by <h2>, etc.) In addition, there should always be an <h1> at the top of a page in order for disabled user to see what that page's content is about.
